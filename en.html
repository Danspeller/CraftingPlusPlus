Crafting++

🎉Welcome to visit the datapack "Crafting++" page! Crafting++ consists of hundreds of items and a skyisland mode. The goal is 

	Few fixed tech trees, more ways to obtain items
	Few fixed manual and arguments, more research project
	Few useful machines, more simple and useless decorations

It mainly contains

  • Elements -- including sky, earth, mountain, dirt, water, fire, life, which corresponds to different mobs, rare drops, force of element and potions. They are used in crafting and rituals.
  • Experience -- used in crafting, machines, tools, wands, rituals.
  • Machine -- All-in-one Machine to grinde, smelt, farm, make stone; Trade Machine to obtain trade automatically; Item Processor to process items; Mob Projector to generate mobs; Beacon Enchancer to provide more effects; Golden Anvil to handle enchantments.
  • Golem -- fishing, farming, mining, herding, killing automatically.
  • Research -- to determine arguments in smelting, rituals for enchanted books, hybridize.
  • Food and decorations -- 41 plants, 82 foods, 79 heads, 21 cloth, 40 spring decorations, 50 pictures, 103 characters, 16 signs, 100 titles, and flags, sheilds.

Create issues if you find any bugs or have any suggestions, please provide the version (Minecraft and Crafting++).

Generation

Flower World

There are only two biomes: flower forests and warm ocean. Dark animals will not generated here.

The time is fixed at noon, no hostile mobs are generated.

In overworld / flower world, put double flowers on an octagon, then put a white wool in the center, the portal will actived. The portal is one-way.

Enchanting Room

Generated on the surface of the flower world, which consists of 12 empty bookshelves, a enchanting table and an illusion.

There are at most 3 stacks of items in every empty bookshelf, which may be papers, books, maps, compasses, random structures maps (exists in this dimension) and ancient scrolls.

Totem Pillar

Generated in the flower world, which is of height 240 and consists of stone_bricks with inner blocks. The chests can contain some Certification of Earth, Nova of Fire, Limb of Ridge, Enchanted Golden Apple, Enchanted Iron, Enchanted Gold, meat floss bread, toffee apple, coal, bones, rotten flesh, gunpowder and string.

Ore Tree

Generated on the surface in the flower world. The sharp is similar to birch trees with spruce logs. The leaves will become ores slowly.

Wool Tree

Generated on the surface in the flower world. The sharp is similar to birch trees with birch logs. The leaves will drop wool saplings, wools, fishes and sheep.

Sakura Tree

Generated on the surface in the flower world. The sharp is similar to birch trees with jungle logs. The leaves will drop sakura saplings and cherries.

Field

Generated on the surface in the flower world, which consists of several crops (wheats, potatoes, carrots or beetroots).

Flowers and Grasses Bushes

Generated on the surface in the flower world, which consists of several flowers or grasses.

Entities

Enhance Mobs

Zombies and skeletons may be armed with better armors and weapons or potion arrows.

Zombie can break torches, lanterns, end rods, sea pickles (instantly) and trapped doors (5 seconds).

The fuse of creepers is modifed to 1.5s. The powered creepers can be spawned.

scoreboard players set $doZombieImproving cppConfig 0 or 1 to disable/enable zombie enchancement.

scoreboard players set $doSkeletonImproving cppConfig 0 or 1 to disable/enable skeleton enchancement.

scoreboard players set $doWitherSkeletonImproving cppConfig 0 or 1 to disable/enable wither skeleton enchancement.

scoreboard players set $doCreeperImproving cppConfig 0 or 1 to disable/enable creeper enchancement.

Shard of the Darkness cpp:shard_of_the_darkness

Some animals become dark animals, there are black particles under their heads. They will close to and attact players. They will drop the shard of the darkness.

scoreboard players set $doDarkAnimalSpawning cppConfig 0 or 1 to disable/enable the generation.

Skyisland

Skyisland Mode会在y=0处为air时自动开启, 建议玩家使用自定义超平坦如minecraft:air;minecraft:flower_forest;进入. Skyisland Mode下主世界不会Raw 成模组的结构, 不同玩家的间距默认为1000米.

玩家首次进入时, 会Raw 成一块基岩 and 一个包含1Dirt, 4Oak Sapling, 16Bone Meal的箱子. The leaves will drop bone meal and strings, 然后参考本模组原版Item合成变动, use strings and 8 sticks to craft snowball.

1.11.2 + 默认不Raw 成单独的岛屿, Input\trigger cppIsland to generate your island.

Place a snow block on a composter to change it to a fermenter. Like the composter, put into plants or foods to get dirt. Put grass on a dirt to get a grass block.

Use snowball and 8 dirt craft 8 Cobblestone. Then craft a furnace, place a snow block on a furnace to change it to a block breaker. Then place block on it and right click the block breaker to break the block. The block will drop:
